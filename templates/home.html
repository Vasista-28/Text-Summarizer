About Us

BERTSUMM

T-BERTSum, based on Bidirectional Encoder Representations from Transformers (BERTs). This is an improvement over previous models, in which the proposed approach can simultaneously infer topics and generate summarization from social texts. First, the encoded latent topic representation, through the neural topic model (NTM), is matched with the embedded representation of BERT, to guide the generation with the topic.
